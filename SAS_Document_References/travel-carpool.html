For Team Members

Carpool etiquette keeps everyone moving on time.

  • Contact your driver and fellow passengers at least 24 hours before travel to confirm arrival times and meeting points.
  • Drivers are responsible for the rental car. No smoking, vaping, or unapproved upgrades.
  • Passengers must contribute to fuel costs. Save receipts—drivers submit them, not passengers.
  • Essential trips only: work site, airport, grocery, pharmacy, meals. Non-essential errands require rider-covered fuel and supervisor approval.
  • If you are delayed, notify your driver and leadership immediately. After 30 minutes without contact, vehicles may depart with leadership approval.
Safety reminder: Wear seat belts, obey traffic laws, and respect quiet hours. Keep vehicles clean and avoid leaving valuables inside.
