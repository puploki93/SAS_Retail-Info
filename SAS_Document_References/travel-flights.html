For Team Members

Review this checklist as soon as you receive your flight confirmation.

  • Confirm the itinerary matches your legal name and preferred airport. Report discrepancies immediately.
  • Check in 24 hours before departure using the airline app or website.
  • Plan to arrive at the airport 1.5 hours before boarding time (you are paid for this window).
  • Keep receipts for baggage fees or parking and email them the same day.
  • Upon arrival at destination, send travel time in the required PROD format to your lead, elite, and supervisor.
Travel Help: If you encounter delays, missed flights, or gate changes, call your project supervisor immediately—do not rely on email alone. After hours, escalate per the travel policy call-outs.
