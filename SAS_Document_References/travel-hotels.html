For Team Members

Keep this information handy when you arrive at the hotel.

  • Present a valid photo ID at check-in. Rooms are direct-billed through CLC; personal credit cards should only be used for incidentals.
  • Do not charge meals, room service, or upgrades to the room without prior approval.
  • No guests are allowed in company-paid rooms. Smoking or vaping is prohibited.
  • Confirm checkout times with the front desk and coordinate with your carpool for departure.
  • Report any issues (lack of reservation, incorrect room type) to the Project Supervisor and Patricia Booker immediately.
